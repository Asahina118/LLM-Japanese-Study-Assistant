## Full Translation

The dictionary is an indispensable tool for learning words. Even if the words are one’s own or foreign, there is no one who has never had the experience of pulling out a dictionary. Dictionaries present words in their original form while also regulating how they should be used; that is, the idea that “ways of using words not found in dictionaries are incorrect” or “must not be used.” In practice, however, it is impossible to record every expression of language activity in a dictionary. While utilizing dictionaries is important, overemphasizing what is written in them can lead to a direction where free use of words becomes regulated, which is undesirable.
